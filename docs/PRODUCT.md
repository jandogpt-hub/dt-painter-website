# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Residential homeowners and commercial property owners in Coral Springs, FL and surrounding South Florida areas. These users are seeking premium quality painting and kitchen cabinet refinishing. They are detail-oriented, value clear communication and craftsmanship, and want beautiful visual proof (like high-quality before/after transformations) to confidently choose a high-trust professional for their property.

## Product Purpose
To showcase DT Painter's elite residential painting, cabinet refinishing, and commercial services. The website exists to build immediate trust, demonstrate master craftsmanship visually, display real-world geographic expertise, and convert high-intent local visitors into scheduled estimate requests.

## Brand Personality
- **Voice & Tone:** Professional, authoritative, pristine, precise, and warm. It speaks with the quiet confidence of an established master craftsman.
- **Three-Word Personality:** Precise, Premium, Craftsman.
- **Emotional Goals:** Instills absolute confidence, a sense of home pride, safety, and excitement for a premium property transformation.

## Anti-references
- Low-budget, cheap-looking contractor websites with poor alignment and generic icons.
- Artificial, clinical stock photography that does not reflect real South Florida homes.
- Flashy startup gradients, neon accents, over-embellished glassmorphic panels, and lazy SaaS grid templates.
- Over-promising, hype-filled copy; avoiding standard "marketing speak" in favor of concrete craftsmanship and honest professionalism.

## Design Principles
- **Show, Don't Tell:** Leverage authentic, high-resolution visual proof of work (such as before-and-after cabinet comparisons) to let the craftsmanship speak for itself.
- **Razor-Sharp Prep:** Every layout, alignment, border, and transition must look as clean and perfectly taped as the edges of a masterfully prepped room.
- **Impeccable Space:** Employ generous whitespace to symbolize the clean slate, cleanliness, and premium execution that DT Painter brings to every job site.
- **Restrained Focus:** High-potency accents (Honey Gold) are deployed strategically to highlight critical trust points (licenses, reviews, primary actions) without distracting from the beauty of the work.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict adherence to WCAG AA contrast standards for all typography.
- Clear, visual focus states for all interactive elements to support keyboard navigation.
- Smooth transitions limited to comfortable speeds (under 300ms), respecting user system preferences for reduced motion.
